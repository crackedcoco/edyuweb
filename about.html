Ed Yu is a photographer and marketer who believes in capturing people in authentic moments.

He currently shoots for Chicago's First Ascent climbing gyms, who feature his work in advertising around Chicago and online. Ed also provides marketing strategy, analytics, and social media management for small businesses.

Ed draws inspiration from his rural upbringing NJ, nature, heavy industry, and cinema. He believes in establishing lasting relationships with his clients, understanding subjects at a personal level, and helping to grow brands. He loves interacting with and capturing images of urban professionals interested in the outdoors.

Ed is based in Chicago and travels around the globe to rock climb. He spends a great deal of time at the Red River Gorge in Kentucky. Inquiring minds may reach him via Instagram or email.
